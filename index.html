The mission

You've seen ten thousand sunrises. You haven't seen one happen to the whole planet at once.

Aurelia exists for a single moment: the instant the sun breaks over the curve of the Earth and dawn sweeps across an entire hemisphere beneath you. Our crews fly it every week. Sixteen times a day if you're counting — one sunrise every ninety minutes, each one arriving like the first. We built the vehicle, the cabin, and the entire voyage around putting you at that window.
